WELCOME TO fiberLikeR

An interactive training tool for forensic science students and practitioners to explore and develop the Bayesian approach to fibre evidence evaluation considering activity level propositions.

fiberLikeR is a knowledge-based system-inspired prototype application that illustrates the integration of current research data with previous research, practitioner experience and analytical findings.

 WHAT YOU CAN DO:

  • Implement the framework of interpretation in assessing textile fibre results considering aspects including transfer, persistence, recovery and background frequency  given activity-level propositions.
  • Formulate propositions for a mock case scenario with alternative sub-scenarios.
  • Consider relevant terms for calculating a likelihoood ratio (LR). 
  • Explore how LR varies depending on how propositions formulated and relevant terms. 
  • Download a summary of input and results for future reference and data exploration..
